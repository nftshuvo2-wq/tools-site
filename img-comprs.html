How to Compress Images Online

  1. Upload your image by clicking the upload area or dragging and dropping your file.
  2. Adjust compression level using the slider to balance file size and image quality.
  3. Select output format (JPG, PNG, or WebP) based on your needs.
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process the image in your browser.
  5. Download the compressed image and save bandwidth and storage space.

Why Compress Images?

Image compression reduces file size while maintaining acceptable visual quality. This helps websites load faster, saves bandwidth, and improves user experience. Compressed images also take up less storage space on devices and servers.

Best Formats for Different Uses

JPG: Best for photographs and complex images with many colors. Offers good compression with adjustable quality.

PNG: Ideal for images with transparency, text, or simple graphics. Uses lossless compression.

WebP: Modern format providing superior compression for both lossy and lossless images. Supported by most modern browsers.
